WHO, WHAT and HOW TO REPORT ABUSE :

Finding out that your child has been abused is the most heartbreaking news a parent can experience. Deciding how to pursue taking legal action is overwhelming, chaotic and stressful. It is confusing who to turn to that fully has you and your childs' best interest.

DO NOT report evidence to your governing sport administration

DO NOT report abuse to the NCAA.

DO NOT report abuse to university police.

DO NOT turn over evidence to ANY OFFICIALS without an attorney of YOUR CHOICE; present.

DO NOT allow your child's sport to appoint you with THEIR LAWYER.

DO NOT speak to any HR personnel without an attorney YOU chose; present.

DO NOT notify your HR club director without an attorney present.

DO : Report to your parents or a trusting adult.

DO : Report to a police station that is un-affiliated with a club/university.

DO : Report to Children's Services.

DO : Contact a lawyer who is NOT affiliated with the university, or has financial ties to the sports club (boosters, scholarships, ect..)
